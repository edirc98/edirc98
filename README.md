## 👾 About Me

I'm a **Unity Developer** based in Barcelona, with 5+ years of experience building interactive applications for VR and games with Unity and C#.

I got into development through games — and I never really left. Today I split my time between professional Unity development in a clinical research environment and building indie games, where I get to experiment freely with mechanics, systems, and ideas.

I care deeply about **clean, maintainable code**: design patterns, SOLID principles, and scalable architecture, whether I'm shipping a research prototype or a personal game project.

- 🎮 Indie game developer — alongside **[mikelghedina](https://github.com/mikelghedina)**
- 🔬 Professional background in clinical VR research at IDIBAPS
- 🏗️ Architecture-first mindset: design patterns, SOLID, clean code
- 📡 Always exploring: AI, computer graphics, 3D printing, new game tech
